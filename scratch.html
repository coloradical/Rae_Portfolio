Vans Ultrarange Project

Capture product photos and videos for social media posts and advertisements to promote the adventure lifestyle of Vans UltraRange sneakers.

October '17

Services: Graphic Design
Deliverables: Product Photos and Videos

Following a 486-mile backpacking trip across the Colorado Rockies in a pair of Vans Hi-tops, I was commissioned by Vans to take photos and videos for their new line of UltraRange sneakers. I was sent Vans branded shirts, shorts and shoes and headed into the mountains to capture the products at work. This new line of sneakers expanded Vans into the outdoor industry by boasting durability and versatility for a variety of different environments, which I showcased during the fall season in Crested Butte, Colorado.

Skating in the Black Canyon of the Gunnison in Vans UltraRange and a makeshift selfie stick for my GoPro.

Portray the adventure and durability of the shoes with a range of shots ranging from "shoe-selfies" to full body.

These videos were montaged with other Vans Ambassador work in a 15 second advertisement displayed on Instagram. The ad recieved over 3.8 million views and featured 4 snippets of my videos adventuring through the mountains of Gunnison and Crested Butte, Colorado. High resolution photos were used for a promotional Instagram post.

Photos captured by me in Vans UltraRange sneakers post featured on Vans Official Instagram in March of 2018.
